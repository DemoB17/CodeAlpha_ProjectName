# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objectives

The purpose of the exploratory data analysis was to understand the structure of the dataset and identify meaningful patterns.

## Analysis Performed

The following analyses were conducted:

- Counted the total number of books.
- Calculated the average book price.
- Identified the highest-priced book.
- Identified the lowest-priced book.
- Counted books in each rating category.
- Calculated the average price by rating.
- Identified the top 10 most expensive books.

## Visualizations

The following visualizations were created:

- Rating Distribution
- Average Price by Rating
- Price Distribution
- Top 10 Most Expensive Books

## Key Findings

The detailed findings and insights will be based on the completed analysis and visualizations.
